Marin Kitagawa

Marin Kitagawa is the charismatic female protagonist of the manga and anime series My Dress-Up Darling (Sono Bisque Doll wa Koi wo suru). She is a lively high school gyaru and model who is a passionate otaku with a deep love for anime, video games, and cosplay.

CHARACTER SUMMARY

Marin Kitagawa is the main female protagonist of My Dress-Up Darling. She is a cheerful high school student, passionate cosplayer, anime fan, gamer, and model. Her open-minded personality and love for cosplay make her one of the most popular modern anime characters.

Surprising Visual & Design Trivia

The Censored Piercing: In the original manga, Marin has a tongue piercing. However, this detail was completely removed from the anime adaptation. Naturally Dark Hair: Her iconic blonde-to-pink gradient hair is entirely dyed. Her natural hair color is actually a standard dark brown. Fake Eye Color: Marin's bright reddish-pink eyes are actually colored contact lenses. Her natural eyes are dark brown, which fans briefly see when she is not wearing her signature gyaru makeup. Industrial Piercings: She loves body modification and sports multiple ear piercings, including a distinct industrial barbell on her left ear.

Food & Lifestyle Quirks

Huge Appetite, Zero Cooking Skill: Marin is a massive foodie who can easily devour a meat bun immediately after a full bowl of ramen. However, her "cooking" usually consists entirely of microwaving frozen meals from convenience stores. Her terrible eating habits are saved only when Gojo begins packing healthy meals for her. The Food Theft Scene: One of her most viral animated moments involves a hamburger buns scene where she accidentally eats Gojo's food, showcasing her chaotic, clumsy nature. Can't Swim: Despite actively planning beach trips and genuinely loving the ocean, Marin is entirely unable to swim or float. Cosplay Community Realism: The series author, Shinichi Fukuda, carefully researched the real-life cosplay community. Marin's dialogue, her obsession with finding the exact fabric, and her financial habits (like saving up paychecks specifically for high-end cosplay cameras) are modeled directly after the habits of real cosplayers.
